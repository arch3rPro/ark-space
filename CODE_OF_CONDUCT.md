# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

ArkSpace should be a practical and respectful project space.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about problems.
- Preserve privacy and avoid sharing private information without permission.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Bad-faith contributions, fabricated claims, or intentionally misleading reports.

## Scope

This code applies to project spaces such as issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and related community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, close, or remove contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from participation.

If you need to report a problem, contact a maintainer through the most appropriate private channel available for the project. If no private channel is available, open a minimal public issue asking for a private contact path and do not include sensitive details.
